Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our development process

?

How much does mobile app development cost?

The cost depends on the project's complexity, number of features, platform (iOS, Android, TV), and design. Simple apps start at a fixed base price, while complex projects are calculated individually after discussing requirements.

?

How long does development take?

On average, development takes from 1 to 6 months. The timeline depends on functionality, integrations, design, and the number of platforms.

?

Do you develop for iOS and Android simultaneously?

Yes. We create native apps for iOS and Android, as well as hybrid solutions that work across multiple platforms at once.

?

Native or Hybrid: which should I choose?

Native development provides maximum performance and flexibility. Hybrid apps allow for a faster market launch and a reduced budget. We will help you choose the optimal variant for your specific goals.

?

Do you help with App Store and Google Play publication?

Yes. We fully manage the publication process, including account setup, app store optimization (ASO), build uploading, and passing moderation.

?

Do you provide post-launch support?

Yes. We offer technical support, updates, bug fixes, and feature evolution after the initial release.

?

Can I order only the app design?

Yes, you can order UX/UI design as a standalone service. We create prototypes, user journeys, and full visual concepts.

?

Do you sign a contract and an NDA?

Yes. We work officially under a contract and sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) to protect your intellectual property.

?

Can the app be integrated with CRM or third-party services?

Yes. We integrate apps with CRM systems, payment gateways, APIs, analytics, and other external platforms.

?

Do you develop apps for Smart TV?

Yes. We create applications for TV platforms, including Android TV and other Smart TV solutions.
